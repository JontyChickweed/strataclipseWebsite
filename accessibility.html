Accessibility

At Strataclipse, we are committed to providing an accessible and inclusive experience for all users. We believe that everyone should have equal access to information and services, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. We continually strive to improve our website's accessibility and usability, adhering to best practices and industry standards.

We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which provide recommendations on making web content more accessible for people with disabilities. Our efforts include ensuring that our website is compatible with assistive technologies, providing clear and concise content, and offering easy-to-use navigation.

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we welcome your feedback. If you encounter any barriers to access or have suggestions on how we can improve our website's accessibility, please contact us.
