Cookie Policy

At Strataclipse, we value your privacy and prioritize providing a transparent browsing experience. As part of our commitment to transparency, we want to inform you that our website does not use cookies.

Cookies are small text files that websites often store on your device to remember your preferences, track your browsing behavior, or provide personalized content. Since we do not use cookies, you can be assured that your browsing activity on our website is not being tracked, and your information is not being collected for advertising or other purposes.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Cookie Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us.
